how to use


1. How do I create a survey?

Go to "Create Survey", choose a survey type, fill in some options, and your survey will be created instantaneously!

2. How do I use my survey with my audience?

Each survey created will have a unique ID or survey tag. In order for people to see the options for your survey, you need to tell people what your survey tag is (each tag will be a combination of your unique User Tag followed by a number...). Then, have your audience members download Class Tempo - Student from the Android or Apple App Store, or have them go to www.classtempo.org/Student.html in order to access the student portal. From there, a student will enter your survey tag to give you real-time feedback!

3. How do I see the results from my survey?

Well, after you create a survey you will automatically be transferred to its results page. If you ever want to go back to that survey, just go to "My Survey Results", and find your survey. It is helpful if you add a quick description to each survey when you create it so that you will have an easier time finding it later.

4. How do I prevent people from tampering with my survey results?

Once you have completed administering your survey, we recommend that you turn it off by going to "My Survey Results", clicking on the survey, and then switching "On" to "Off". This way, people will not be able to answer your survey after your audience has finished.

5. What prevents people from responding more than once to my survey?

Glad you asked! Your audience will be unable to fill out your surveys more than once because Class Tempo uses a smart system to detect when a user has already taken your survey, and will be prevented from taking it more than once.

6. Where do I go from here?

The information provided here should be enough to get you whipping up surveys like a storm, but if you have other questions or suggestions feel free to email support@classtempo.org. Thanks for joining our mission to improve the classroom!
