Why use ClassTempo?

  • It's absolutely free.

  • Flexibility

    Create surveys anywhere with any device that can access the Internet (i.e. laptop, tablet, Blackberry, Android phone, iPhone, etc...) Class Tempo is also available as an app on the Apple App Store and the Android Marketplace.

  • We support Firefox, Google Chrome, Safari, Internet Explorer 9, and Opera

  • Improve Learning

    Class Tempo allows teachers to regulate the pace at which they teach, making sure that students understand the most material possible.

  • Participation

    Students can participate in class while remaining anonymous. Students often have questions that they are afraid to ask in front of a large audience; Class Tempo lets students ask a teacher these questions right from their laptop or smartphone.

  • Real-time Results

    View the results for your survey in real-time for our Analytics Page. We provide simple and understandable graphs to interpret your results.
