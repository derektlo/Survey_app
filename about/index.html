Class Tempo

What is Class Tempo?

Class Tempo is a web-based mobile app designed to innovate the classroom. Teachers have been teaching in a similar way for generations, even though techonology has progressed immensly. We need to bring technology into the classroom, and not just fancy projectors or smart-lights. Technology needs to better the education of students. Class Tempo does just that.



Who's using Class Tempo?

Class Tempo is being used by professors at the MIT Sloan School of Management, and has had great success.

The head computer studies teacher at the Concord Academy High School is also using Class Tempo for his lectures.
