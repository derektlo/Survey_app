Survey Type

    Select Survey...

  • Classic

    Create a classic survey with custom options.

  • Student Response

    Receive text responses from your class. (Example Survey: Ask students if they have any questions. They might be afraid to ask a question in front of their peers but comfortable asking it through ClassTempo!)

  • Tempo

    Regulate the speed of your class. While on, this survey will allow students to send an alarm if they feel you are teaching too fast. After a certain percentage (customized by you) of students send alarms, the app will beep.
